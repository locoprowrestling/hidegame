## Prompt

Pixel art game sprite, 64×128 pixels, transparent background, PNG with alpha.
Retro first-person shooter enemy sprite style — Wolfenstein 3D / DOOM era.
Hard aliased pixels, flat colour banding, no anti-aliasing, no gradients, no brush strokes.

The Games Master: tall imposing villain, facing the viewer directly, standing upright.

**Pose:** Weight evenly on both feet, slightly wider than shoulder-width stance.
Left hand grips a black walking cane planted vertically on the ground, the silver skull
pommel at hand height. Right arm hangs relaxed at his side, fingers loosely curled.
Head faces forward, centred. Completely still — the stillness of a man who does not need
to hurry.

**Suit:** Perfectly-fitted black two-button suit. Black trousers with a clean crease.
Black dress shirt, collar open. Suit folds shaded in banded pixel art — three tones:
near-black (#0d0d0f) for deep shadow, dark charcoal (#1e1e22) for mid-tone, dark blue-grey
(#2c2c34) for highlight planes. Jacket lapels visible. Single jacket button fastened.

**Shoes:** Polished black leather dress shoes. High-gloss spot highlight in pixel art —
two or three bright pixels on the toe cap (#2a2a30 over #141416 base).

**Mask:** Red lucha libre skull-demon wrestling mask covering the entire head.
Mask base colour #cc1a1a. Black pixel markings — jagged crack lines radiating from eye
holes, flame shapes on crown. Eye holes are dark sockets (#7a0e0e interior) with two
small bright red glow pixels (#ff2200) — the eyes.

**Gloves:** Red skeletal gloves — crimson bone-line markings (#cc1a1a) over black glove
fabric (#141418). Finger-bone lines visible on the back of the right hand.

**Cane:** Black shaft (#181818), one pixel wide, running from grip down to ground.
Silver skull pommel at the top — small round skull shape, 4–5 px across, metallic grey
(#9a9a9a) with dark socket marks (#585858).

**Figure proportions within 64×128 canvas:**
- Skull pommel: y 2–6
- Mask top: y 4–8
- Mask base / collar: y 22–26
- Shoulders: y 22–28, x 6–58
- Jacket body: y 26–80
- Waist: y 76–84
- Trouser legs: y 84–118
- Shoe soles: y 118–126
- Canvas floor: y 127–128 transparent

**16 colours maximum.** Figure must read clearly as a silhouette even at 8px tall
(far-distance rendering). Dominant read: tall dark suit, bright red mask.

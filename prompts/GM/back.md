## Prompt

Pixel art game sprite, 64×128 pixels, transparent background, PNG with alpha.
Retro first-person shooter enemy sprite style — Wolfenstein 3D / DOOM era.
Hard aliased pixels, flat colour banding, no anti-aliasing, no gradients, no brush strokes.

The Games Master: tall imposing villain, seen from directly behind, mid-stride walking away.

**Pose:** Back facing the viewer. Walking away at a measured, unhurried pace — left foot
forward, right foot back (mid-stride). Right hand holds the cane, planted or slightly
trailing. Left arm swings gently back. Head is straight — the back of the red lucha mask
is visible: smooth crimson dome with the rear strap or seam down the centre, and the tail
end of black flame markings curling from crown to back.

**Back of the suit:** The jacket back is a clean dark panel — centre seam visible as a
1-px dark line. Shoulder seam lines across the top. The jacket vents (split at the base
of the jacket back) are slightly open from the stride, showing 2–4 px of dark trouser
beneath. No lapels visible from behind. The jacket's cut and quality reads even from the
back — this is an expensive suit.

**Back of the mask:** The rear dome of the red lucha mask. Crimson-red (#cc1a1a) curving
away from the viewer. A faint seam or lacing line runs centre-back (1-px dark line,
#7a0e0e). Black flame markings continue from the front — partial flame tips visible at the
sides and crown of the back. No eye holes visible — just the smooth red dome.

**Gloves:** Right glove visible, gripping the cane. The red bone-line markings are on the
back of the glove hand — partially visible since the hand faces partially away. Left glove
at the back-swing of the left arm — may be partially obscured by the jacket.

**Cane:** In the right hand, trailing slightly. Cane planted or near-planted. Skull pommel
at the top of the cane, facing away — back of the silver skull visible (rounded dome,
#9a9a9a) rather than the face.

**Figure proportions within 64×128 canvas:**
- Mask back dome top: y 4–8
- Mask base / collar: y 20–24
- Jacket shoulders: y 20–26, x 8–56
- Jacket back panel: y 26–78
- Jacket vent / trouser tops: y 74–84
- Trouser legs (striding): y 84–120
- Shoe soles / heels: y 118–126

**16 colours maximum.** The dominant silhouette reads: wide dark shoulders tapering to
trousers, red dome of mask at top. The figure should feel like it is moving away with
total indifference — the GM does not need to look back.

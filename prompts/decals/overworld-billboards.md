# Overworld — Street Billboards & Plaques (4 decals)

> 48 × 64 px, pixel art, PNG with alpha. See style-guide.md for all hard rules.

Hand-placed flanking the three entrances and in storefront windows on Main
Street (positions in OW_DECALS, decals.js). Seen at night under moonlight —
keep contrast in big shapes.

---

## 1. ow_billboard_a — `assets/decals/ow_billboard_a.png`
Wooden poster board, fresh playbill behind glass: red TONIGHT band (one huge
readable word), and a black-and-white sepia playbill for **"THE PHANTOM AT THE
OPERA HOUSE"**. The featured Phantom is modeled after The Vigilante reference at
`/Users/gecko/Library/Mobile Documents/iCloud~md~obsidian/Documents/LoCo Pro Wrestling/Characters/TAS-VIGILANTE.png`:
an athletic male wrestler in a deep hood, black eye mask, short beard and curled
moustache, sleeveless dark ring gear, wrapped forearms, and a bold upward-pointing
chest emblem. Translate those traits into the hard-edged limited-palette style;
do not copy the bright green color scheme. Keep the existing monochrome
black/white/sepia print treatment and red title accents. The only "new" object
on the street.

## 2. ow_billboard_b — `assets/decals/ow_billboard_b.png`
Same board weathered: older playbill torn diagonally (alpha on the torn area,
board wood showing through), sun-bleached, red band faded to rust. Whatever
show it advertised, the date bars are scratched out.

## 3. ow_hotel_plaque — `assets/decals/ow_hotel_plaque.png`
Polished brass plaque on stone: engraved HOTEL IMPERIAL bars, EST. date line.
Centered small, plenty of transparent margin. Catchlight along the top bevel.

## 4. ow_mill_notice — `assets/decals/ow_mill_notice.png`
Official paper notice, rain-stained: heavy black KEEP OUT band (one huge
readable phrase), condemnation text-bars, a wax seal blot lower right.
Stains bleed from the top edge.

# Round 2 — Sugar Mill Signage (4 decals)

> 48 × 64 px, pixel art, PNG with alpha. See style-guide.md for all hard rules.

Works notices in the beet yard, wash house, slice floor, evaporators. Period
industrial: stencils, chalk, company paper. Everything slightly damp.

---

## 1. mill_safety — `assets/decals/mill_safety.png`
Buff card safety notice with a red **"SAFETY FIRST"** header and the exact goal
**"GOAL: 15 DAYS INJURY FREE"**. Below it is a month-style grid of exactly
30 square checkboxes, arranged 5 rows by 6 columns. Exactly 4 boxes contain
check marks; the other 26 contain large X marks, showing that only four days
were injury-free. Keep every box large enough to read after reduction. Add a
black/yellow hazard chevron strip across the bottom.

## 2. mill_tonnage — `assets/decals/mill_tonnage.png`
Wood-framed chalkboard, dark green-black slate: chalk header, a ruled tally
grid of daily tonnage figures. The last column's entries degrade into frantic
scrawl. Chalk dust ghosting of erased older numbers.

## 3. mill_missing — `assets/decals/mill_missing.png`
Grey company paper, bold dark-red MISSING band at top (one readable word, huge).
Centre: a washed-out photo square of a face gone almost featureless. Text-bars
below. Corners pinned; one pin gone, corner curling (alpha).

## 4. mill_sugar — `assets/decals/mill_sugar.png`
Faded enamel advert: pale blue field, proud white sugar sack with a red brand
band, cheery slogan text-bars. The optimism of it should feel wrong in situ.
Chipped enamel at the edges (alpha nicks).

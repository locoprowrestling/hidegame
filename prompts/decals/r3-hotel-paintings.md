# Round 3 — Hotel Imperial Paintings & Boards (4 decals)

> 48 × 64 px, pixel art, PNG with alpha. See style-guide.md for all hard rules.

Hung in the lobby, ballroom, guest corridors. Gilt and mahogany pretension,
long past its era. Every painted face shares the same red pin-prick eyes —
guests notice it on the second look, not the first.

---

## 1. hotel_portrait_a — `assets/decals/hotel_portrait_a.png`
Gilt-framed portrait, edge-to-edge (no alpha): a pale woman in high-collared
lace, hair severe, face porcelain-flat. Two pin-prick red eyes. Varnish
crackle bands.

## 2. hotel_portrait_b — `assets/decals/hotel_portrait_b.png`
Gilt-framed portrait: a heavy-set man, muttonchops, dark suit against a darker
ground — almost swallowed by it. Red pin-prick eyes. One corner of the frame
visibly broken.

## 3. hotel_hunt — `assets/decals/hotel_hunt.png`
Framed nocturnal landscape: black treeline under a low bone-coloured moon,
empty field in the foreground — except one tiny figure in red standing in it,
facing the viewer. 2–3 px tall. Most viewers will never spot it. Good.

## 4. hotel_rates — `assets/decals/hotel_rates.png`
Mahogany tariff board with brass rules top and bottom: ROOM RATES header bars
in brass paint, neat rows of text-bars and prices. The bottom line is newer
paint, hastily added — slightly crooked.

# Round II — Win Screen (screen-win-r2)

> **320 × 200 pixels. PNG. No alpha.**
>
> **STYLE: Pixel art. Hard edges. No anti-aliasing. No gradients.**

The win screen displayed when the player escapes the Sugar Tower roof with all 40 punch cards.

**Scene:** The rooftop of the Great Western Sugar Works factory at night. The player has
just emerged through the hatch and is standing on the flat factory roof.

**Sky:** Very dark blue-black (#04060e) occupying the top 60% of the image. Stars: scattered
single-pixel bright points (#c8ccd8), about 20 stars total, irregularly placed. One star
slightly brighter (3 px bright cluster near upper-right).

**Horizon:** The dark prairie of Longmont at night — a flat horizon line (y≈120) in near-black.
Faint amber glow just above the horizon to the left (#2a1808) suggesting the distant town lights.

**Factory rooftop:** The lower 80 px of the image. Dark concrete parapet walls (#1e1c18)
running left and right from the central hatch opening. Ventilation stacks: 2-3 dark cylindrical
shapes rising from the roof surface (#141412), each 8-12 px wide and 20-30 px tall. Sugar
crystal accretion on the parapet edges: pale off-white crystalline crust (#b0ae a8) along
the top edge of the parapet walls.

**The phantom locomotive:** In the lower-right quadrant of the image, at the horizon,
a Great Western Railway steam locomotive is visible at distance — rendered very small
(roughly 40 px wide, 18 px tall) but with clear pixel art detail: dark iron body (#1a1814),
a faint amber light from the firebox (#4a2808), white steam billowing from the stack
(pale grey-white pixel clusters, #b0b0b0, drifting upward and left). The locomotive faces
left (heading toward the factory). One bright headlight (2×2 px, #e0e0d0) cuts through
the dark.

**Signal flare:** Just above the roof surface near the centre of the image: a bright
signal flare streak — a near-vertical trail of bright orange-white pixels (#ffa030 →
#ff4010 → #200808) rising 30 px from the player's position, suggesting the escape signal
just fired.

**Mood:** Dark, wide, lonely — but the locomotive is coming. Cold Colorado night sky.
The factory is finally behind the player.

**Note:** This image is used as a background — game engine overlays text and gradients on top.
Keep the composition clear in the centre for text overlay.

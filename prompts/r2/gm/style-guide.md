# Round II — Games Master in the Sugar Works

The Games Master character is identical to Round 1 — same body, same suit, same mask,
same cane. He does not change costume or design between rounds. All sprite files from
Round 1 (`gm-idle.png`, `gm-walk-a.png`, `gm-walk-b.png`, `gm-lunge.png`, `gm-back.png`,
`gm-back-a.png`, `gm-back-b.png`) are reused unchanged.

See [Round 1 GM style guide](../../GM/style-guide.md) for full character specification.

---

## Narrative context (for reference, not visual change)

In Round 1 the Games Master operates his territory in the Opera House — he is at home,
unhurried. In Round 2 he is out of place in the industrial darkness of the sugar factory.
The same black suit against corrugated iron and crystalline walls reads differently —
more alien, more wrong. This contrast is achieved through environment design alone; the
character sprite does not change.

His patrol behaviour is the same. His catch distance and detection range are the same.
The "night superintendent" framing is narrative only.

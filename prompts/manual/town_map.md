> **STYLE: antique surveyor's town plat / bird's-eye map, ink and wash on

A hand-drawn bird's-eye plan of a small fictional Colorado downtown at night,
drawn on stained parchment (#cdbd9d aged to brown at the edges, vignetted dark).

Layout (matches the in-game overworld):

- A wide **main street running vertically** through the centre, drawn as
  cobblestone hatching, with a **cross avenue** running horizontally midway.
- **Upper-left block:** a grand opera house drawn in bird's-eye — pitched roofs,
  a fly tower. Marked with a small **crimson wax seal**.
- **Upper-right block:** a tall hotel with a light ray drawn from one window.
  Marked with a **gold wax seal**.
- **Lower-right:** an industrial mill complex with a smokestack inside a fenced
  yard, gate on the west side. Marked with a **dark wax seal**.
- **Lower-left:** ordinary storefront blocks, drawn plainly, unsealed.
- Tree symbols ringing the map border; a few scattered in a small park.
- A compass rose, bottom-left, drawn in faded red ink.
- Dotted ink footpath lines connect the three sealed buildings.
- In the street between the avenue and the mill: a **tiny inked silhouette of a
  standing figure with a cane**, casting a long shadow the wrong direction.
- Scrawled illegible annotations (loops and dashes that suggest handwriting,
  zero readable letters) near each sealed building, as if someone catalogued
  them in a hurry.

**Dominant tones:** parchment tan and sepia ink, three wax-seal accents
(crimson #a01818, gold #c0a060, near-black), darkness pooling at the corners.

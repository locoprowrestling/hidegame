A night street scene in a small Colorado downtown, 1980s-meets-Victorian: a
brick Main Street receding into darkness under a starry, moonless-dark navy sky
(#0a0c1c). Three buildings dominate, lit from within while everything else is
dead dark:

- **Left:** a grand stone opera house, four storeys, tall arched windows — two
  windows glowing warm amber (#c08030). Crimson double doors at street level.
- **Right:** a brick grand hotel, six storeys, one window per floor faintly lit,
  a glowing transom light over a mahogany entrance.
- **Far background, low between them:** the silhouette of an industrial sugar
  mill with a tall stack against the sky.

**Centre of the street, mid-distance:** a lone figure standing perfectly still,
facing the viewer. Tall, thin, black Victorian suit, a cane planted in front of
him with both gloved hands resting on its skull-shaped pommel. His face is a
**deep-red skull mask** (#a01818) with hollow black eye sockets — small at this
distance but unmistakably looking at you. A faint red rim-light haloes him,
sourceless.

Wet cobblestone foreground reflecting the amber window light in long streaks.
Heavy vignette into near-black at all four corners. Mood: an invitation you
should not accept.

**Dominant tones:** near-black browns, aged gold, one deliberate accent of
blood red on the mask and doors.
